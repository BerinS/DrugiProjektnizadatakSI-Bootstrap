ABOUT Movie News & Reviews

Movie News & Reviews is an online database of information related to films, television series, and streaming content online – including cast, production crew and personal biographies, plot summaries, trivia, ratings, and fan and critical reviews. Movie News & Reviews began as a fan-operated movie database in 2022. It is now owned and operated by me- a Mario, Inc., a subsidiary of Srebrena Malina. As of March 2022, the database contained some 6.9 million titles (including television episodes) and 42.0 million person records. Additionally, the site had 8008 million registered users. The site's message boards were disabled in February 2017.
